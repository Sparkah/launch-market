# Bilt.me Mobile Companion Brief

## App Name

Game Factory Launch Market

## Audience

- Creators who want to submit a playable game idea from a phone.
- Backers who want to inspect evidence before assigning launch credits.
- Tim as the operator approving promotion into the real Factory.

## Tabs

### Create

- One prompt field.
- One allocation rule field.
- Submit button: `Create candidate`.
- After submit: show job id, spec status, and next validation stage.

### Market

- Candidate feed.
- Each card shows fairness score, launch score, business score, missing evidence,
  and whether Sui/Walrus/DeepBook are live or mocked.
- Allocation action appears only after evidence status.

### Portfolio

- User's watched candidates.
- Testnet launch-credit allocations.
- Outcome status: sandbox, proof uploaded, promoted, rejected.

## Payment Story

- Free: 1 free build, then plays mint more (30 plays = 1 build).
- Creator Pro: $25/month, 5 builds a day (matches the web pricing).
- Studio: $50/month, 25 builds a day, for studios and clients.
- Marketplace: 5-10 percent take-rate on revenue-share games promoted through
  the market.

## Real-World Signal

- Existing Game Factory pipeline and gallery.
- Existing catalog of HTML5 game prototypes.
- Existing need: decide which AI-generated games are safe and worth promoting.
